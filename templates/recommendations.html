General Study Tips

Pomodoro Technique

Study for 25 minutes, then take a 5-minute break. After 4 cycles, take a longer 15-30 min break.

Sleep & Memory

Sleep 7-8 hours. Your brain consolidates memory during deep sleep — don't skip it before exams!

Spaced Repetition

Review material at increasing intervals: day 1, day 3, day 7, day 14. It dramatically improves retention.

Active Recall

Don't just re-read notes. Close the book and try to recall key points. Testing yourself beats passive reading.

Move Your Body

Short 10-min walks between sessions improve focus and reduce cortisol (stress hormone).

Prioritize Ruthlessly

Always start with the highest-priority subject when your energy is fresh.
